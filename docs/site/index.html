NetMon

Real-time home + mobile network dashboard.

Polls your UniFi gateway, Peplink routers, and ping targets every few seconds. Pairs with the iOS app over your LAN.

Download for macOS iOS App (App Store)

Requires macOS 13+. Signed & notarized. Free & open source.

1. Install the server

Download the DMG, drag NetMon.app to Applications, then launch it. It runs as a menu-bar app — click the icon to see the server URL and pairing QR code.

2. Pair your iPhone

Install the iOS app from the App Store, tap "Scan QR", and point it at the menu-bar QR code. Pairing stays on your LAN — no cloud account.

3. Watch your network

Your devices appear on the dashboard as cards. Configure alerts, thresholds, and layouts in the iOS app's Settings tab.
